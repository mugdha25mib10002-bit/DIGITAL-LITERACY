<h2>Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist: </h2>
<h3>✅ DO’s:
1 Use strong and secure passwords
  
2 Keep your profile professional

3 Think before posting anything

4 Verify information before sharing

5 Respect others online

❌ DON’Ts:

1 Do not share personal or sensitive information

2 Avoid posting inappropriate content

3 Do not spread fake news

4 Avoid cyberbullying

5 Do not click on unknown or suspicious links</h3>
