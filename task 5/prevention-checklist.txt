Prevention Checklist

Never share your passwords, OTPs, or bank details with anyone
Always double-check the sender before trusting any email or message
Avoid clicking on links that look unknown or suspicious
Make sure the website is secure by checking “https://” and the correct URL
Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for better security
Use strong passwords and change them from time to time
Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept payment requests from strangers (UPI safety)
Always check the receiver’s name carefully before making any UPI payment (UPI safety)
Try to avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or important activities
If something seems suspicious, report it immediately to the cybercrime helpline (1930) or website (cybercrime.gov.in)
