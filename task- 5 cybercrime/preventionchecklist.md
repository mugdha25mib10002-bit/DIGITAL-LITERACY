<h2>Prevention Checklist</h2>

1.Never share your passwords, OTPs, or bank details with anyone.


2.Always double-check the sender before trusting any email or message.


3.Avoid clicking on links that look unknown or suspicious.


4.Make sure the website is secure by checking “https://” and the correct URL.


5.Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for better security.


6.Use strong passwords and change them from time to time.


7.Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept payment requests from strangers (UPI safety). . 8.Always check the receiver’s name carefully before making any UPI payment (UPI safety).


9.Try to avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or important activities.


10.If something seems suspicious, report it immediately to the cybercrime helpline (1930) or website (cybercrime.gov.in).
